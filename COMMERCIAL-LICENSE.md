## 1. When you do **not** need this

You are already covered by the free [CC BY-NC 4.0](LICENSE) license — no payment,
no separate agreement — when **all** of these hold:

- You use the skills to run **your own** business or automation, for your own income.
- You keep the attribution and a link back to this repository.
- You do **not** redistribute, resell, repackage, or sublicense the pack itself.

Running the skills in the background of your own operations counts as your own use,
even if your customers benefit from the result. That is the intended use.

## 2. When you **do** need this

You need a commercial/OEM license when the skills stop being *your internal tool*
and become *part of a product or service you sell*, in particular:

- **Embedding / OEM** — the skills (or material derived from them) ship as a
  component inside a service, app, or product you charge third parties for.
- **Attribution waiver** — you want to remove the required credit entirely.
- **Redistribution** — you want to distribute the pack, or a modified version,
  to your own users or customers.

## 3. Tiers

Both tiers are an **annual, non-exclusive** license that **includes ongoing
updates** for the term, scoped to **one named service**.

| Tier | What it covers | Attribution | Price |
|------|----------------|-------------|-------|
| **Standard OEM** | Embed in one named commercial service | Kept on a credits / NOTICE / licenses page — **never required in your end-user UI** | **US$180 / year** |
| **White-label OEM** | Standard OEM, plus a full attribution waiver | None required anywhere | **US$480 / year** |

**One-time perpetual (optional):** either tier is available as a one-time
perpetual license at ~2.5× the annual fee (**$450** / **$1,200**). A perpetual
license does **not** include updates released after the purchase date.

## 4. Terms

Every commercial license, unless the written order says otherwise:

1. **Scope** — one named service or product. A second service needs a second license.
2. **Non-exclusive** — the author may license the same rights to anyone else.
3. **Non-transferable & non-sublicensable** — the license is yours; you may not
   assign it or pass the rights through to a third party.
4. **No reselling the pack** — you may not resell, republish, or repackage the
   pack itself, or distribute it as a standalone product, under any tier.
5. **No competing product** — you may not use the material to build or market a
   competing skill pack, template library, or equivalent product.
6. **Attribution** — required per your tier (§3). A waiver applies only if your
   order is the White-label tier.
7. **Updates** — annual licenses include updates during the paid term. Perpetual
   licenses are frozen at the purchased version.
8. **Term & renewal** — annual licenses run 12 months and renew by mutual
   agreement. Lapsed annual licenses end the grant (see §9).
9. **Termination** — the license terminates automatically on material breach
   (e.g. reselling the pack, exceeding scope, or non-payment) and on non-renewal
   of an annual term. On termination you must stop distributing the affected
   service with the material embedded within 30 days.
10. **No warranty** — the material is provided "as is", without warranty of any
    kind. The author's total liability is limited to the fees paid in the prior
    12 months.
11. **Governing law** — New South Wales, Australia, unless the order states
    otherwise.
